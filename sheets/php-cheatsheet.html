PDO & Datenbank
Verbindung
$pdo = new PDO( 'mysql:host=localhost;dbname=mydb', $user, $pass, [PDO::ATTR_ERRMODE => PDO::ERRMODE_EXCEPTION] );
Prepared Statement
$stmt = $pdo->prepare('SELECT * FROM users WHERE id=:id'); $stmt->execute(['id' => $id]); $row = $stmt->fetch(PDO::FETCH_ASSOC);
Niemals Variablen direkt in SQL-Strings einbauen — immer Prepared Statements!
